Arcade Fusion

You are Jax, a lightning-fast arcade hero. Run, jump, glide, collect pellets, place bombs, dodge ghosts, smash crates, grab food and deliver orders before time runs out.

🎮 Gameplay Mix Platformer movement + flap boost + bombs + pellet chains + ghost chases + delivery missions.
🕹️ Controls Keyboard: ← → move, Z/Space jump, X flap, C bomb. Mobile: use the touch buttons below.
🏆 How to Win Deliver 3 orders, survive waves, defeat the final boss and enter the portal with max score.
⚡ Scoring Pellets grow your combo, bombs clear groups, orders give big bonuses, the portal ends the stage.
Tip: chain pellets quickly for multiplier and save bombs for crowd control or the boss.
